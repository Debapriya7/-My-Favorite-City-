About the City

Kolkata, the capital of West Bengal, is known as India's "Cultural Capital," famous for its rich heritage, literature, arts (cinema, drama), vibrant festivals like Durga Puja, delicious Bengali cuisine, historic colonial architecture, and unique atmosphere blending grandeur with grit, all supported by iconic yellow taxis and India's oldest metro system. It was the former capital of British India, offering a distinct cultural experience compared to other Indian metros, with a strong sense of identity despite historical challenges.
